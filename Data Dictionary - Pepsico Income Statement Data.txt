Pepsico Income Statement Data


This dataset contains 5 variables and 86 observations. The dataset describes the total standardized revenue by quarter from the second half of 1993 to the last quarter of 2014. The dataset was created and cleaned by Jacob Rose using data from Mergent Online linked here 
https://www-mergentonline-com.ezproxy.bu.edu/companyfinancials.php?pagetype=standardized&compnumber=6581&period=Quarters&dataarea=PL&range=50&currency=AsRep&scale=AsRep.


report_date – Date that the data was reported and recorded
year – Year of data recorded
quarter – Quarter of the data recorded, disregarding year
revenue – Total standardized revenue for the quarter listed
time – individual number counting down representing time from start of dataset
